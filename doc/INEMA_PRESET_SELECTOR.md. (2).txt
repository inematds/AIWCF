# INEMA.CLUB — Experience Preset System

## Overview

INEMA.CLUB uses a **single structural and content framework** with **multiple experiential presets**.

The structure and message remain constant.  
What changes is **how the experience feels, moves, and expresses energy**.

To enable this, the system introduces an **Experience Preset Selector**, allowing users to switch visual and motion styles in real time.

---

## Experience Preset Selector

**Location:** Top-right corner of the interface  
**Type:** UI toggle / dropdown / segmented control  

**Label:**  
> Experience Mode

**Behavior:**
- Changes **visual accents**, **motion rules**, and **interaction intensity**
- Does **not** change structure or content
- Presets apply globally to the site

---

## Baseline Preset (Reference)

### Preset 0 — Premium Calm (Default)

**Purpose:**  
Institutional, timeless, premium baseline.

**Visual**
- Dark base
- Minimal color accents
- Subtle glass
- Wide spacing

**Motion**
- Fade + translateY (8–12px)
- Duration: 0.6–0.8s
- Almost no background motion
- Simple hover states

> Luxury through restraint.

---

# Active Presets

Each preset defines **visual style + motion behavior**.

---

## Preset 1 — Youth Energy

**Personality:**  
Youthful · Curious · Alive

### Visual
- Animated gradients (blue → purple → cyan)
- Bright accent highlights
- Gradient headlines
- More contrast between sections

### Motion
- Entrance: scale (0.95 → 1) + fade
- Duration: 0.4–0.5s
- Strong stagger between elements
- Background blobs or waves (slow loop)
- Hover: glow + scale (1.02)

> The interface feels alive and playful.

---

## Preset 2 — Momentum

**Personality:**  
Action · Progress · Execution

### Visual
- Strong directional lines
- Section dividers
- Minimal decorative background

### Motion
- Entrance: slide from X-axis
- Duration: 0.3–0.4s
- Aggressive stagger
- Scroll-driven progression
- Click feedback: compress + bounce

> Motion communicates forward movement.

---

## Preset 3 — Flow Learning

**Personality:**  
Continuity · Focus · Deep learning

### Visual
- Soft gradients
- Connected sections
- Fewer visual breaks

### Motion
- Entrance: fade + morph
- Duration: 0.8–1s
- True parallax layers
- Continuous background flow
- Minimal hover effects

> Scrolling becomes part of the learning narrative.

---

## Preset 4 — Future Impact

**Personality:**  
Visionary · Provocative · Futuristic

### Visual
- Neon accents
- High contrast typography
- 3D grids, particles, light beams

### Motion
- Entrance: mask reveals / clip-path
- Duration: 0.5–0.6s
- Section state changes on scroll
- Active particle backgrounds
- Dramatic hover effects

> Motion is spectacle and statement.

---

## Preset 5 — Human Pulse

**Personality:**  
Empathy · Connection · Warmth

### Visual
- Warmer accents (coral, lilac, soft blue)
- Organic shapes
- Softer contrast

### Motion
- Entrance: organic fade + scale
- Irregular timing (less mechanical)
- Slow, natural background motion
- Hover: gentle lateral movement

> Motion feels human, not mechanical.

## System Rule

**What NEVER changes:**
- Site structure
- Educational message
- Core pillars of INEMA.CLUB

**What ALWAYS changes with presets:**
- Color accents
- Motion rules
- Interaction feedback
- Visual rhythm

---

## Final Statement

> INEMA.CLUB has one structure, one message, and multiple experiential modes.

This system allows:
- Personalization without fragmentation
- Energy without chaos
- Motion without losing clarity

**Structure stays fixed.  
Experience adapts.**
