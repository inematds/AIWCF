
INEMA.CLUB — The One Prompt Website Formula
Education for Transformation in the AI Era

--------------------------------------------
OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------

This document defines the official INEMA.CLUB website creation model using a single structured prompt.
The focus is education, digital transformation, and AI literacy, guiding users from knowledge to action and empathy.

INEMA.CLUB is built on three core pillars:
1. Communication with Machines
2. Entrepreneurial Automation
3. Human Behavior

Transformation only happens when these three pillars work together.

--------------------------------------------

INEMA.CLUB

├── CTA
└── FOOTER

--------------------------------------------
2. BRANDING GUIDELINES
--------------------------------------------

1. Visual Identity — "Knowledge, Clarity & Glass"

- Typography:
  Use Inter or SF Pro Display.
  Headlines should be large (56–72px), clear, and authoritative.
  Body text should be readable, calm, and high-contrast.

- Color Palette:
  Deep Space Gray background (#0d0d0d)
  Off‑White primary text (#f5f5f7)
  Subtle blue and violet gradients representing intelligence and learning.

- Shapes:
  Soft Squircle corners (24–30px radius).
  Friendly but precise geometry.

- Materials:
  Light glassmorphism using backdrop-filter: blur(16–20px)
  Thin borders (1px, low opacity)

2. Motion — "Clarity in Motion"

- No aggressive animations
- Smooth ease-out curves
- Subtle parallax for learning flow
- Scrollytelling to guide understanding

3. Mobile & Quality

- Mobile-first learning experience
- Large touch areas
- App-like navigation
- Visual calm and focus

--------------------------------------------
3. CONTENT INSTRUCTIONS
--------------------------------------------

1. HEADER (Navigation)

Content Inputs:
- Logo Text: INEMA.CLUB
- Navigation: Home, Pillars, Programs, Community, Contact
- Mobile Menu: Hamburger (auto)

Content Elements:
- Floating glass navigation bar
- Uppercase links, small size, wide spacing
- Logo aligned left

Background:
- Frosted glass with subtle transparency

Style References:
- Apple.com navigation
- iOS Control Center glass

--------------------------------------------
2. HERO SECTION

Content Inputs:
- Headline (H1):
  "Education for Transformation in the AI Era"

- Subtitle:
  A short sentence about accelerating knowledge, action, and empathy.

- Primary CTA:
  "Start the Journey"

Content Elements:
- Calm, authoritative hero
- CTA glass button

Background:
- Abstract light gradients
- Dark base with subtle motion

--------------------------------------------
3. PILLARS SECTION

Content Inputs:
- Pillar 1: Communication with Machines
- Pillar 2: Entrepreneurial Automation
- Pillar 3: Human Behavior
